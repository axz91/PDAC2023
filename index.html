Human Pancreatic Ductal Adenocarcinoma (PDAC) Muscle Transcriptome

Introduction

The Atlas provides a comprehensive view of the gene expression landscape in the PDAC patients, encompassing both mRNA and miRNA transcriptomic data. This invaluable resource serves researchers, clinicians, and drug developers working in the field of cancer cachexia, offering insights for the development of novel therapeutic strategies and improved patient outcomes.

Authors:

Hsi Chang, Ashok Narasimhan, Teresa A. Zimmers

Citation

Ashok Narasimhan, Xiaoling Zhong, Andrew R. Young, Sha Cao, Jun Wan, Sheng Liu, His Chang, Leonidas G. Koniaris, Teresa A. Zimmers. Sex- specific transcriptome and microRNAome in human skeletal muscle in pancreatic cancer cachexia. Submitted, 2023.
